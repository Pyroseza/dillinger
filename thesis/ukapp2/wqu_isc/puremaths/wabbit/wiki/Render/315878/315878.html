struct TIApplication

This structure holds the information stored by the calculator for each application in its flash memory.

Members

  • BSTR Name

Name of the application, no more than 8 characters long.  It is padded with space characters up to 8.

  • IPage Page

The first page of the application.  The pages are stored on the calculator in descending order.

  • int PageCount

The number of pages in the application.
